Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information that applies to our visual communication platform (the "Service"), and your choices about the collection and use of your information. We collect information you provide when registering for an account or corresponding with us, including:

b) Information from Third-Party Applications

When you access our Service through third-party connections (Facebook, Google, Discord), we may receive:
